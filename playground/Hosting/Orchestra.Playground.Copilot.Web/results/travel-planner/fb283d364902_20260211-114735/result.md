# Oregon 2-Night Trip Itinerary

## Day 1: Portland Exploration

### Morning (9am-12pm)
- **Powell's City of Books** – World's largest independent bookstore
- Walk the Pearl District galleries and boutiques
- Coffee at Stumptown Coffee Roasters
- **Cost:** Free (books optional $20-50) | **Transport:** Walking

### Afternoon (12pm-5pm)
- **Lunch:** Portland Food Cart Pods – try various cuisines ($10-15)
- **OMSI** – Interactive science museum + submarine tour (2-3 hours)
- **Cost:** $37 ($22 admission + $15 lunch) | **Transport:** TriMet MAX ($2.50)

### Evening (5pm-9pm)
- Walk along the Willamette River waterfront
- **Dinner:** Alberta Arts District restaurants ($25-40)
- **Dessert:** Voodoo Doughnut ($5)
- **Cost:** $35-45 | **Transport:** Uber ($15)

**Day 1 Total: ~$80-130** (excluding accommodation)

---

## Day 2: Columbia River Gorge Day Trip

### Morning (8am-12pm)
- Grab coffee and breakfast to-go ($10)
- Drive to **Multnomah Falls** (30 min) – hike to Benson Bridge viewpoint
- Scenic drive on Historic Columbia River Highway
- **Cost:** $15 ($5 parking + $10 breakfast) | **Transport:** Rental car ($65/day)

### Afternoon (12pm-5pm)
- Hike **Wahclella Falls** or **Latourell Falls** (1-2 hours)
- **Lunch:** Thunder Island Brewing in Cascade Locks ($15-20)
- Drive to **Vista House** at Crown Point for panoramic views
- **Cost:** $20 | **Transport:** Rental car (included)

### Evening (5pm-9pm)
- Return to Portland (1 hour drive)
- **Dinner:** Craft brewery in Pearl District ($30-40)
- Evening stroll, optional dessert
- **Cost:** $35-45 | **Transport:** Walking

**Day 2 Total: ~$135-145** (including car rental, excluding accommodation)

---

## Day 3: Mt. Hood & Departure

### Morning (8am-12pm)
- Early breakfast at hotel or nearby café ($15)
- Drive to **Timberline Lodge** (1.5 hours) – explore the historic lodge
- Short alpine hike or enjoy mountain views
- **Cost:** $15 | **Transport:** Rental car (half-day ~$35)

### Afternoon (12pm-3pm)
- **Lunch:** Timberline Lodge dining room ($20-30)
- Drive back to Portland, return rental car, head to airport
- **Cost:** $60 ($25 lunch + $35 car) | **Transport:** Rental car → airport shuttle

**Day 3 Total: ~$75-90**

## Quick Reference

### Accommodation
| Level | Area | Price/Night |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Budget | Downtown/Old Town | $80-120 |
| Mid-range | Pearl District/Alberta Arts | $150-220 |
| Luxury | Nob Hill/Waterfront | $300+ |

### Transport
| Mode | Cost |
|------|------|
| TriMet day pass | $5 |
| Uber/Lyft | $15-25/ride |
| Car Rental | $50-80/day |

### Local Tips
- No sales tax in Oregon
- No self-serve gas (attendants pump)
- Tip 18-22% at restaurants
- Bring cash for some food carts
- Bring layers – gorge can be windy
