2. **Valuation Disconnect**: Trading at 24x forward P/E (3-year low) vs. 17% growth; historical premium re-rating potential

4. **Fortress Fundamentals**: $89B cash, $77B FCF, 39% margins provide massive cushion

### 🐻 Bear Case
1. **AI Execution Risk**: Copilot adoption slower than expected; enterprise ROI unproven
2. **CapEx Burden**: $37.5B/quarter spend (66% increase) pressuring near-term margins
3. **OpenAI Concentration**: 45% of backlog tied to single partner creates dependency risk
4. **Multiple Compression**: If AI hype fades, premium valuation may not return
5. **Competitive Pressure**: Open-source AI models could commoditize Microsoft's advantage
